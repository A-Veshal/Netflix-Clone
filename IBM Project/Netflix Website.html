Frequently Asked Questions

  • Netflix is an American subscription video on-demand over-the-top streaming service. The service primarily distributes original and acquired films and television shows from various genres, and it is available internationally in multiple languages

  • As of 2024, Netflix offers the following subscription plans in India:
    1. Mobile: ₹149 per month (limited to mobile devices)
    2. Basic: ₹199 per month
    3. Standard: ₹499 per month
    4. Premium: ₹649 per month

  • You can watch Netflix on a wide variety of devices. Here are some common options:
    Smart TVs: Most smart TVs have a pre-installed Netflix app.
    Streaming Devices: Devices like Roku, Apple TV, Amazon Fire Stick, and Google Chromecast.
    Gaming Consoles: PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch have Netflix apps available.
    Computers: Through web browsers on Windows and macOS.
    Mobile Devices: Smartphones and tablets running iOS and Android using the Netflix app.
    Set-top Boxes: Many cable and satellite boxes support Netflix.
    Blu-ray Players: Some models come with Netflix pre-installed.

  • To cancel your Netflix subscription:
    1. Log in to your Netflix account.
    2. Go to "Account" settings.
    3. Select "Cancel Membership" under the "Membership & Billing" section.
    4. Confirm your cancellation.
